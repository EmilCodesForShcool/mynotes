# Plot
- Hector and Molly are assigned to work in a food truck together to both serve customers and explore the woods.
- Molly grows sus about what Hector is doing after their shift. He's often tired in the morning and his tiredness increases by the day. He dozes off during cooking and almost caused a fire. This is usual for an experienced chef like him.
- Molly was woken up by a bad dream one night featuring Hector. He was surrounded by unknown monsters that are attacking him. She could not find Hector anywhere in their truck. She starts driving into the woods.
- Molly felt like someone was telling her directions on where to drive, every turn felt calculated
- She made it to Hectors location. He's wounded, **badly**. Molly checks on him
- They ran back to their truck, Molly was starting the keys then.
- Banging was heard on the truck wall! The beasts were attacking the truck! Panic sets in!
- A deer appeared in the headlights. There was a flash, then monsters are gone? They hear a voice. It's similar to the voice that told Molly directions to Hector.
