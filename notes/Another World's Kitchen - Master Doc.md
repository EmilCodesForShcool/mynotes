## LORE - CHARACTERS

## The cooks

Master

- Master hunter and chef, founder of The Pavilion, helps people that are in danger due to the area's beasts. Take in chefs to train them and they help him run his restaurant.

- As the years go by, he gets weaker due to age and is not as powerful of a hunter as before. He attacked but lost to a just a simple deer...? He is currently alive but barely.

- The Master has had more students before but they all died trying to surpass him, he currently has 3 students left: Hector, Molly and William. They both study his craft and skills along with help him run his restaurant - The Pavilion.

- He currently resides in the upper floors of The Pavilion, leaving the restaurant to his students.

- Old man, face mostly covered in bandages, one eye present, lots of scars from hunting trips. He rests most of the time but does come out to test dish quality and provide feedback for his students

The Pavilion

- Considered the civilization's most prestigious cooking academy.
- A restaurant dedicated to serving local people of the civilization fresh, delicious and nutritious food.
- Ingredients sourced by their own chefs so they can pick what's best to eat. Normally include monster meat, fresh wild forages but also produce grown in the Pavilion's farm.

The first lady

- The master's wife, responsible for both caring for the master along with his students at the end of the day.
- Old cozy lady, makes the best food (according to the students)
- The Master teaches while the First lady cares for his students
- Resides in the upper floors of the Pavilion along with the Master.

Hector - The Butcher

- Current head of the Pavilion. Mostly stoic, calm and collected, charismatic. Super angry if he loses his shit. High-key perfectionists and a bit overbearing + likes being passive aggressive to mask his anger.
- Most well rounded cook and skilled in combat - the Master sees him as his own son + his pride
- His parents died from a monster attack, he was rescued by the Master and put under his care when he was very young.
- He has a twin brother that died protecting him. He still has trauma from that event

- Personal info:

    - Height: 169 cm
    - Age: 21
    - Eye color: Who knows? this guy never open his eyes
    - Preferred weapons: bladed weapons (swords, daggers, knives and axes)

Molly - The Angler

- Vice head, feisty, short-temper but very kind and straightforward. Cares for those around her in her own ways + customers don't really like her generally.
- Great eyesight, good angler but is rather messy and careless which makes it hard for her to work in the line with others (she normally gets a corner in the restaurants kitchen). Good combat techniques and strategies but her body limits her from getting bigger prey (smol)
- Came from a family of anglers, many of whom died while being under the Master's care, she made it the longest among all family members. She went to the Pavilion due to pressure from her family to become the best in their bloodline.

- Personal info:

    - Height: 152 cm
    - Age: 21
    - Eye color: Green and hazel
    - Preferred weapons: Pole arms and crossbows

William - The Baker

- Silly, giggly like a kid but is very sympathetic to others. Customers love his baking and hates his jokes. Dad joker. Shows signs of Williams' syndrome
- Baking with the ability to turn people into rats, the other cooks thinks that he's not human but he doesn't harm anyone so they don't care. The baked goods that can turn people into rats have a special powder put into them. Can talk to rats and have an army of them that help the Pavilion ( they're clean dw). Can fight but just doesn't. The cooks just accepted this and let him be, he runs a farm instead that was started by the First lady. The First lady loves him.
- Came from a sea family that assimilated himself into human culture and has a human disguise
- He can't eat seafood, most people just think that he's allergic

- Personal info:

    - Height: 190 cm
    - Age: 21
    - Eye color: Sapphire
    - Preferred weapon: Doesn't like to fight, but if he has to, he'll use his legs since his hands are only for baking.
